Rivayd Tovar

Job: Master Technician Position: Owner

Rivayd Tovar was born in Mexico City, Mexico. Upon his arrival to the United States he began his career as a mechanic and for the past two decades he has served the Monmouth County community. Currently residing in Wall Township he plans on taking his expereince to you!

In the beginning of his career he worked for General Motors company before branching out and opening his own business. He opened Auto Amigo Service Center on Route 35 in Ocean Township and further expanded his experience and knowledge while working there. Finally, after 20 years as a mechanic he currently brings his knowledge of import and domestic vehicles to Body Motion Body Shop as a Master Technician.
